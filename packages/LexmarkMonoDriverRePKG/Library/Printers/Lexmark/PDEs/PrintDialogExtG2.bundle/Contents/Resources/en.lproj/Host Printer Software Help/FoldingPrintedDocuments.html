Folding printed documents

Note: Make sure that a fold finisher is installed in your printer. For more information, see the printer User’s Guide.

  1. In the options menu, select Finishing.

  2. In the Fold menu, select how you want to fold a document.

    • Off—Disable the fold feature.

    • Fold—Fold up to five sheets in half automatically, short edge to short edge, to form one bundle. For print jobs larger than five sheets, each five-sheet bundle is nested in the next bundle.

    • Fold and Center Staple—Fold the document in half automatically, short edge to short edge, and then staple along the fold.

      Note: Make sure that a staple finisher is installed in your printer. For more information, see the printer User’s Guide.

    • Fold Individual Pages—Fold individual sheets in half automatically, short edge to short edge.

    • TriFold Individual Pages—Fold individual sheets in thirds automatically, short edge to short edge.

    Note: These options are available only in some printers.

  3. Click Print.
