Setting the print resolution

This feature lets you adjust the resolution of the printed output in dots per in (dpi), or in color quality (CQ). Increasing the print resolution increases the quality or sharpness of the printed output.

  1. In the options menu, select Quality.

  2. In the Resolution menu, select a resolution.
